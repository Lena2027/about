Brückentage 2026: How to Maximize Your Vacation Days

In Germany, 'Brückentage' (bridge days) are the secret to a long vacation without using all your annual leave.

What is a Brückentag?

A bridge day occurs when a public holiday falls on a Tuesday or Thursday. By taking the intervening Monday or Friday off, you create a four-day weekend while only using one day of leave.

Strategy for 2026

2026 offers several excellent opportunities for bridge days. For example, Ascension Day always falls on a Thursday. By booking the following Friday off, you get a significant break in May.
